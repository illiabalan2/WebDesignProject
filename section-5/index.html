Ancient Egypt

Ancient Egypt is known to be one of the oldest civilizations. It has a history of over 5000 years and is vastly known for its amazing architecture, culture, art, etc. The civilization thrived greatly along the banks of The river Nile. It provided them with fertile soil for agriculture which was the absolute opposite of the otherwise harsh desert environment. From the beginning of the civilization, around 3100 BCE, from the first Pharaoh being successful in unifying the country, to the conquest of Alexander The Great in 332 BCE, the Egyptian civilization has left an impending mark on the world's history. From the pyramids, which are considered to be architectural marvels to the mummies, hieroglyphic writings, and the religious culture. Even more remarkable is the science, spirituality, and philosophy blended into a lasting legacy. The ancient Egyptian civilization was one of the oldest of the Mediterranean World, from around 3100BC till the time of the conquest of Alexander the Great in 332 BC. The ancient Egypt had its history divided into different time periods.
