The 1916 Rising

  • The 1916 Easter Rising was a pivotal event in Irish history, where Irish republicans launched an armed rebellion against British rule in Dublin. Led by the Irish Volunteers and the Irish Citizen Army, the rebels seized key locations in the city, declaring an Irish Republic. Though the uprising was suppressed within a week, it marked a turning point, galvanizing public support for Irish independence and leading to the eventual establishment of the Irish Free State.
